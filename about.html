About me

As a mother of two young children, I decided to shift gears and pursue my passion for web development.

I have a background in sales and marketing, but now I'm fully dedicated to coding. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, SQL, and PHP. Currently, I'm diving deep into Javascript, React, Python, Django, and DRF.

I love writing code and can't wait to share my work with you!
