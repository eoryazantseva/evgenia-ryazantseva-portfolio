About me

I am a web developer based in Brisbane, Australia, with a diverse background that spans the globe. Originally hailing from Russia, I've embarked on a worldwide journey alongside my husband, residing in locations as diverse as Zambia, the United States, and the remote Australian outback.

The adventure of motherhood, with two young children, inspired me to take a new career path and follow my passion for web development. While my professional roots were in sales and marketing, I have wholeheartedly shifted my focus to coding. Proficient in HTML, CSS, SQL, and PHP, I have also delved into advanced technologies such as Javascript, React, Python, Django, and DRF, successfully applying them in various projects.

Coding is not just a job; it's my passion. I take great pleasure in writing code and am eager to share my work with you.

Whether you're looking to translate your vision into reality or seeking a collaborator on your next project, I'm here to help bring your ideas to life!
